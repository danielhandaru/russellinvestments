What is your tax file number?*


By providing my TFN, I agree for it to be used as outlined in the TFN Collection Statement.
Why is it important to give us your Tax File Number?

Under superannuation law, we can collect your Tax File Number (TFN) for lawful purposes. While you don’t have to give us your TFN, doing so means:

  • we’ll be able to accept all types of contributions to your account(s);

  • the tax on contributions to your super account(s) will not increase;

  • other than the tax that may generally apply, no additional tax will be deducted when you start drawing down your super benefits and;

  • it will make it much easier to trace different super accounts in your name, so that you receive all your super benefits when you retire.

The Trustee of your super fund may share your TFN with another super provider when your benefits are being transferred, unless you ask the Trustee in writing that your TFN not be shared with any other super provider. Your TFN will otherwise remain confidential.

Can't find your TFN? Call the Australian Taxation Office on 132 861.
